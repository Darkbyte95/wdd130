History

Rapids White Water Rafting began as a small family business in 1995, driven by our passion for the river and the desire to share that adventure with others. What started with a single boat and a couple of paddles has grown into one of the most recognized rafting companies in the country. Over the years, we have taken thousands of people on thrilling adventures, always committed to safety, fun, and environmental conservation.
